## Data Set - (EDA)

seaborn car crashes https://github.com/mwaskom/seaborn-data/blob/master/car_crashes.csv 

**car crashes**

This dataset contains automobile insurance and fatal collision statistics for 50 U.S. states and the District of Columbia (51 total records). Each row represents one state (or D.C.), and the columns describe driving behavior factors and insurance-related costs.

**Dataset Size**

| ITEM | DESCRIPTION |
|-------|------------|
| Name | Car Crash Data sheet|
| Source | seaborn data repository|
| records | 51 rows & 7 coloums|

**Column Descriptions**

| column | Description |
|----------------------|
|speeding | Percentage of drivers involved in fatal collisions who were speeding |
|------------------------------------|
|alcohol | Percentage of drivers involved in fatal collisions who had a blood alcohol level above the legal limit |
|----------------------------|
|not_distracted | Percentage of drivers in fatal collisions who were not distracted at the time of the accident |
|-----------------------------|
| no_previous | Percentage of drivers involved in fatal collisions who had no previous accidents or violations |
|---------------------------------|
| ins_premium | Average annual car insurance premium in the state (USD) |
|----------------------------------|
| ins_losses | Average insurance losses per insured driver in the state (USD) |
|-----------------------------------|
| abbrev | Two-letter postal abbreviation for the state|

**Summary**

This dataset enables analysis of relationships between driving behavior risk factors (such as speeding and alcohol involvement) and automobile insurance premiums and losses across states.
